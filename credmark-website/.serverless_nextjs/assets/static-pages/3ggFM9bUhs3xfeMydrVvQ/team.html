Neil Zumwalde I Founder

An Electrical/Software Engineer, Neil has developed products and provided technical advisement in blockchain, industrial automation, and renewable energy markets. To Neil, nothing is impossible.

Momin is Credmark’s secret weapon. He is versatile, a quick study, and usually in a good mood after 10AM. Besides being responsible for the development of Credmark’s projects, Momin is the author of our Crypto Credit Report.

Born in Argentina, Paul has lived and worked all over the world. He used to write a lot of code. Now he’s kind of rusty. He does whatever needs doing, whether or not it’s fun.

Emilie Yeager I CTO

Emilie Yeager is a dev ops pro, engineering manager, and DIY aficionado. For the past 20+ years, Emilie has worked in tech- from systems administration to engineering to devops. Her specialties include AWS, Terraform, Bash, and Python automation.


In her spare time, she enjoys the Austin music scene, rock climbing, two-stepping, and adding to her exotic plant collection.

Stijn Dobbelaere I Designer

From editing national ad campaigns for Capital One, to creating viral-ready memes for Southwest Airlines, to voicing Pizza Hut commercials, Stijn has years of experience in design, advertising and marketing.

Collier Gray I Strategic Operations

A native Austinite, Collier spent 6 years in technology and finance in NYC, working from Product to leading a Lean Governance group.
