CREDMARK COMMUNITY UPDATE - AUGUST 2021

Hello Credmarkers!

July was a GREAT month. We released our first consumer product, published research, brought and great new advisor to the project, and watched our community continue to grow.

CREDMARK PRESENTS: SMARTPOOL!

We did it! We had a limited beta release of our first consumer product, Credmark SmartPool. SmartPool makes using Uniswap v3 easy.

Compared to v2, v3 is a beast. Swapping is still easy, but providing liquidity is daunting. SmartPool makes it easy to create risk-adjusted liquidity pools. All you need to decide is how much risk you’re willing to take. The tool does the rest.

We launched with 4 pools across 5 assets, and will be adding to that number every week.

If you haven’t checked it out yet, the beta is available by signing up for our discord.

CREDMARK TOKENOMICS

Every month we’ll update you on our Uniswap fees generated and harvested. Our CMK/USDC pool on Uniswap is set to 1% fee structure. This allows for us to provide price support and reduce circulating supply by locking CMK in the Treasury.

Right now the circulating supply is 10.1m CMK.

The other 89.9m CMK is held by the Credmark DAO, locked in the Community Treasury, and locked in Vesting Contracts for presale investors, team members, advisors, and founders.

This month, we collected

  • 674 USDC,
  • 0.02 ETH, and
  • 10,339 CMK

from our Uniswap pools during liquidity harvesting.

The USDC was reinvested into the pool.

The ETH was used for gas fees.

50% of the CMK was sent to the DAO and 50% was locked in the community treasury for eventual distribution.
